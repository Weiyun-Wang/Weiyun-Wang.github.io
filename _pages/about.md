My name is Weiyun Wang, a PhD candidate in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Glasgow, under the supervision of Dr. Xianghua (Sharon) Ding and Dr. Ilyena Hirskyj-Douglas. I graduated from the Academy of Fine Arts of Tsinghua University in 2022 with a master's degree, and before that, I graduated from the Luxun Academy of Fine Arts with a bachelor's degree. With a background in design and fine arts, my research focuses on leveraging tangible interactive devices to facilitate healthy living in everyday life.
